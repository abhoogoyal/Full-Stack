Frequently Asked Questions

What is TravelMatrix?

TravelMatrix is a travel website where you can book flights, hotels, and tour packages easily.

How do I book a tour?

Go to the 'Tour Packages' page and click on the 'Book Now' button under your desired package.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking. Check the cancellation policy on the booking page.

Do you provide international tours?

Yes, we offer both domestic and international tour packages.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is optional and can be added while booking a trip.
